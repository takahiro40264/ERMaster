ビュー 各顧客の注文合計金額

物理名:
customer_order_summary

SQL:
SELECT
    c.id,  
    c.name,
    c.mail_address,
    SUM(od.order_number) AS total_orders_amount
FROM
    customer_master c
JOIN
    order_data o ON c.id = o.customer_id
JOIN
    order_detail od ON o.id = od.order_id
GROUP BY
    c.id, c.name, c.mail_address;
